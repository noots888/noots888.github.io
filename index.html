Pay Rates

Rates

Used by Earnings. Tax is estimated automatically using Australian resident annual tax brackets + 2% Medicare levy, annualised from the Sunday-to-Saturday pay cycle. Rates save automatically as you type.

Pay Rules Used By Earnings
  • Pay cycle: Sunday to Saturday, based on the currently selected diary date.
  • Tax: estimated by annualising this pay cycle gross, applying Australian resident 2025–26 tax brackets, then adding an estimated 2% Medicare levy.
  • Normal hours: 8.28 hours per paid normal day. A non-RDO week is 41.4 hours; a Rostered Day Off week is 33.1 hours unless the RDO is worked or moved.
  • Weekday/Saturday overtime: first 2 hours at 1.5× base rate, remaining overtime at 2× base rate.
  • Sunday overtime: all overtime at 2× base rate.
  • Public holiday not worked: pays normal hours automatically and does not reduce the paid week like an RDO.
  • Public holiday worked: pays normal hours plus all worked job time as public-holiday penalty, estimated at 2× base rate.
  • Rostered Day Off: unpaid/zero normal hours in the weekly total unless moved or worked. RDO moves are read from Settings → Rostered Day Off.
  • On-call: weekday on-call uses the on-call rate entered above.
  • Allowances: LAHA, inconvenience, higher duties, general allowance, and nightshift are counted once per selected day.
  • Nightshift: marks the calendar with a moon symbol and adds the nightshift rate to the pay cycle.
  • Pay Centre: compares expected vs actual pay, breaks down OT and public holidays, flags fatigue, and lets you compare payslip gross/net after payday.
This is an estimate for field tracking, not a payroll/legal tax statement. Payroll may differ for deductions, award rules, leave loading, super, HELP/HECS, salary sacrifice, and employer-specific agreements.

Compliance Checker

Faults conditions

Warning-only checker based on the Faults Conditions table. Availability means on-call, and on-call is not counted as rest.

Rules checked

14h shift/call-out cap, 120h rolling 14-day cap, max 4 consecutive night shifts, 10h rest between shifts, 2 × 24h or 48h rest per rolling 14 days, nightshift rest rules, no split shifts, and on-call availability blocked during rest.

Best accuracy needs job start/finish times. If times are missing, the checker will warn instead of pretending it knows.
